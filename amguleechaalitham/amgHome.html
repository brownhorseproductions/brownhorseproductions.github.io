Dear viewers

This little film was the start of a journey that many of us who worked on it have dreamt of for long. For me, it was over two decades. All this while, I've been watching, dreaming, observing, following, discussing, reading and occasionally writing about cinema yet never daring to create it. Having taken this baby step, I look back and realise this is the only way it could have happened. Thanks to all who have inspired, supported and helped in this journey.

A few years ago, I came across this wonderful movie which impacted me profoundly. As a student of business and cinema, it answered many questions and opened windows to a new world. Though my film doesn't hold a candle to Nina's, let me use the same words she did,
